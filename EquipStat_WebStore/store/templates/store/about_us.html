Hello there, we are co-founders of EquipStat. We have come here with one goal and that is to help students. Our company is by the students, for the students, and to the students. We believe that a student, must not waste their parents hard earned money on stuff like Lab coats and Drawing instruments which are going to be used only in one semester and never again for most of the students. So, we at EquipStat provide the students with two options either to buy new instruments and calculators at subsidised rates from us or choose from a variety of refurbished options available for instruments and books. We hope that this small effort from the EquipStat team will help students save some money and their valuable time in searching for these products. On that note, I would also like to invite all the second year students who would wish to enlist their products on our platform so that you too can help the freshers and also get some reimbursement back. We have done our bit, now its your turn.
Welcome to EquipStat.
